About Us

Who We Are

Crypto World, is an initiative by a group of individuals, for offering blockchain training in multiple domains that help professionals march towards their goal. Power-packed with young technology enthusiasts and research scholars, our mission is to embrace game-changing technology and bring qualitative changes to the world.


What we do

With many educational collaborations,we aim to explore the disruptive potential of blockchain technology for achieving public good. We offer online and offline blockchain training for blockchain fanatics. The technology-driven institute promotes research, development, and entrepreneurship to spur blockchain adoption by leading enterprises and student communities.


Where We Are

Since 2016, We have been serving as an exploratory research lab driven by curious and pioneering spirits. We are an Associate Member and Official Training Partner of the Linux Foundation Hyperledger Project. Backed by global mentors, trusted international partners, we stand tall in offering Blockchain Certification Programs, R&D activities, and Consultancy Services.
